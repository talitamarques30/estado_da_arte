About guides and possible defect


When you see a very small object in the sky and you want to show it to whoever is next to you, you point and tell the person to look, but they can't see it. You keep pointing and reinforcing the fact that there is something there, you point in the right direction, you get upset because the person still can't see the same thing as you. And this repeats itself, sometimes until the other person starts to think you're crazy seeing random things in the sky. But the person doesn't have the same point of view as yours. She's in another. So you have the idea of ​​moving closer to where that person's eyes are to try to point in a closer direction, which they could be able to align. After a long time of this affliction, the person finally sees what you are seeing. And she will never see it the same way you do.
